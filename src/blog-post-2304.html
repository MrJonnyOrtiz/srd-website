Modernizing your home: ideas for a contemporary remodel.

If you're looking to modernize your home and give it a fresh new look, a contemporary remodel may be just what you need. A contemporary remodel can update your home's style and increase its functionality while creating a more comfortable and inviting living space. Here are some ideas to consider when modernizing your home with a contemporary remodel.

  1. Open Concept Design: Contemporary design often features an open concept layout, which removes walls and barriers to create a more expansive and connected living space. This can be achieved by opening up the kitchen to the living room, removing walls between adjacent rooms, or creating a large central living area. At Sarasota Remodeling, our designers will measure your space and provide you with a completely redesigned floor plan of an open concept in your home.

  2. Minimalism: Contemporary design embraces minimalism, using clean lines and simple shapes to create a sleek and sophisticated look. To achieve this, consider decluttering your home and getting rid of any unnecessary furniture or decor. Use neutral colors and textures, such as white, beige, or gray, to create a calming and inviting atmosphere. Our designers will work with you to achieve that minimalistic look and feel.

  3. Lighting: Contemporary design often incorporates natural light and modern lighting fixtures to create a bright and airy living space. We can install large windows or skylights to let in natural light, as well as modern light fixtures such as pendant lights, track lighting, or LED strips to illuminate your home's interior.

  4. Smart Home Technology: A contemporary remodel can also incorporate smart home technology, such as smart thermostats, lighting systems, and security cameras. These features can help you control your home's temperature, lighting, and security from your smartphone or tablet, making your home more convenient and energy-efficient.   5. Updated Appliances and Fixtures: One of the most important aspects of a contemporary remodel is updating your appliances and fixtures to modern, energy-efficient models. This includes upgrading your kitchen appliances, installing new bathroom fixtures, and replacing outdated light fixtures and ceiling fans. We can install all the latest modern appliances that will update your home.

  6. Sustainable Materials: Contemporary design also emphasizes sustainability and eco-friendliness. We can install sustainable materials such as bamboo flooring, recycled glass countertops, and energy-efficient windows and doors to create a more environmentally-friendly living space to reduce your carbon footprint.

Modernizing your home with a contemporary remodel can create a more comfortable, functional, and stylish living space.
